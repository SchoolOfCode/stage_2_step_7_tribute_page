Steve Jobs - Father of Apple

What happened during the life of Steve Jobs...

This is a short tribute page about Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple, and a timeline from his birth to his sad demise at the age of 56, due to his health. An interesting story of innovation, success, competition and a life which made a hugh dent in the universe. For more info click here

Timeline for his life

  • 1955 - Born Feb 24th 1955 in San Francisco. Later adopted and interested in engineering and electronics from an early age
  • 1968 - Was given an internship by Bill Hewlett for the summer
  • 1970 - Met Steve Wozniak, who is the co-founder of Apple
  • 1974 - Worked as a technician at Atari, created a game called Pong
  • 1976 - Released first product the Apple 1
  • 1977 - Apple becomes incorporated and released the Apple 2
  • 1980 - Went public and raised $110 million, becoming the most successful public stock offering at the time
  • 1981 - Apple's computer 'Lisa' failed even though it was given priority over Macintosh
  • 1984 - Launched the Macintosh. See the famous advert directed by Ridley Scott here
  • 1985 - Steve Jobs resigned from Apple after a power struggle with the then CEO John Sculley
  • 1986 - Jobs buys, what was soon to be known as Pixar, and released films such as Finding Nemo, Bugs Life and Toy Story
  • 1996 - After some strong competition from Microsoft, leadership changed hands at Apple and purchased NeXT for the OS Jobs' and his team had developed
  • 1997 - Steve Jobs became interim CEO of Apple
  • 2000 - Apple have now released the iMac, iPod, iPad etc under the leadership of Steve Jobs granting him the permanent CEO position
  • 2003 - At this point Steve Jobs had been diagnosed with pancreatic cancer
  • 2007 - The first iPhone was released by Jobs, offering many innovations and cloud offering
  • 2008 - Sad health speculations continued but Jobs attempted to keep them quiet
  • 2011 - On October 5th, CEO Steve Jobs passed away at age 56, after a long battle with pancreatic cancer
