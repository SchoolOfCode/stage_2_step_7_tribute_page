Christopher Nolan

Biography

Christopher Nolan was born in London on the 30th of July 1970. He is known for his Hollywood blockbusters with complex storytelling, Nolan is considered a leading filmmaker of the 21st century. His filmography includes:

  • Memento
  • Insomnia
  • Batman Begins
  • The Prestige
  • The Dark Knight
  • Inception
  • The Dark Knight Rises
  • Interstellar
  • Dunkirk
  • Tenet
